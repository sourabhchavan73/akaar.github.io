About Us

About Ankar

Aakar Jewellery Studio is one of the finest jewel makers based in Mumbai. Our world-class artisans have over 30 years of experience in the craft. Our services are specially catered towards people looking to get custom designs for their ornaments. Aakar has many unique aspects about it; Antique gold collection, a lifetime worth of maintenance and repairs, at home service which allows you to try our jewellery at the comfort of your home and best buyback guarantee and exchange policy.

Vision / Mission

Jewellery is used for the most important & rare moments in our life. At Aakar we strive to create the perfect jewel that reflects who you are to make you shine on your special occasion. Each piece of ornament that we create is as unique as the customer. In the end, all we hope to achieve is shaping your imagination into reality.
